The best comic book movies according to Shaurya

My top 3 comic book movies of all-time

Avengers: Infinity War

Amazing cinematography, direction and story. The ending was spectacular and the pacing was perfect.
It's so rewatchable and it got me so hyped for Endgame.

Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice - Ultimate Edition

Now, don't get me wrong. The BvS theatrical cut is pretty bad. However, the additional 30 minutes make
a real difference. We get so much more depth and it is genuinely the best DCEU film (ZSJL comes close).
The CGI and cinematography are beautiful. Ben Affleck was an oustanding Batman and Gal Gadot portrayed
Wonder Woman flawlessly. The only problem I had was the pacing. However, it doesn't outperform Infinity
War, in my opinion.

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

Without a doubt the greatest animated film of all time, this sequel builds upon the strengths of its
predecessor. The animation is on point, with some amazing scenes, especially the Spider-Verse vs Miles
scene. Metro Boomin made an amazing soundtrack and I could listen to it again and again.
